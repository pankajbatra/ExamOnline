Getting Started With ExamOnline

Please read the steps below carefully to get started.

Step 1.

Please click on the 'My Test Center' link on the left hand side menu of the control panel to take a new test.
  

Step 2.

 Choose the desired test from the list of test that appears.
 

Step 3.

 Please  read the instructions carefully and then begin your test.
 

Step 4.

 After successfully completing the test the score will be displayed.
 

Step 5.

 You can also view the scores of all the tests you have taken so far by using the 'My Score Card' link.
 

Step 6.

 You can change your registration information using the 'Change Account Information' link.
 

Step 7.

 You can also change your password through the 'Change Password' link.We suggest you to change your password atleast once a week to avoid any security lapses.
