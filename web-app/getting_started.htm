Getting Started With ExamOnline

Please read the steps below carefully to get started.

Step 1.

The first time when you log onto the control panel there will be no available tests in your account as you have not configured any tests.
  

Step 2.

 Please click on the 'Configure New Test' link on the left hand side menu of the control panel to configure a new test that you wish to conduct.
 

Step 3.

 Please fill up the forms that appear by reading the instructions carefully.
 

Step 4.

 After successfully configuring the test the test will be available to you in the control panel.
 

Step 5.

 The control panel will allow you to perform actions such as 'Start Test' , 'Stop Test' , 'Delete Test' , 'Add,Edit and Delete Questions'.
 

Step 6.

 The control panel will also provide you with details such as the 'Test Name' , 'Test Code' , 'Date Of Creation' , 'Date Of Updation' , 'Test Duration' and the 'Test Status'.
 

Step 7.

 You can also change your password through the 'Change Password' link.We suggest you to change your password atleast once a week to avoid any security lapses.
